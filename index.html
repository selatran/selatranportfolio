Biography

Sela Tran is a Seattle based graphic designer who is currently working towards her BA in design and a UX certificate at EWU. Her work is influenced by Swiss Style graphic designers whose work focuses on functionality and grid designs. Sela has always been interested in drawing and photography as a child and it was in high school that she discovered graphic designing. Since then she has experimented with photography, typography, and product/motion/web/UX desing. Her goal is to one day create a positive impact through her work. Besides design, Sela enjoys learning new languages and hopes to one day travel the world to experience different cultures.
